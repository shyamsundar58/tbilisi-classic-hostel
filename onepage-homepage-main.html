About Tbilisi Classic Hotels

Tbilisi Classic Hotels is built to support international students who want a smooth start to studying in Tbilisi, Georgia. For over a decade, we’ve helped students with the complete education migration journey—education consultancy, university admission support, and residence procedures—so they can settle in with confidence.
